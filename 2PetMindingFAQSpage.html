Q: How do we know you have had a Police check?
A: A copy of the Police check confirmation will be provided with booking confirmation.

Q: We haven’t made a booking, is it too late?
A: Just give us a call on 1300 697 380 and we can check the calendar.

Q: Our dog needs medication morning and night, will this be possible?
A: Yes, medication giving is included in basic services if that is your dog’s daily need.

Q: If our dog rolls in something ‘yuck’ while on a walk, will you bath and dry him before leaving?
A: Certainly!! My own dog gets a bath if he’s rolled in ‘yuck’ before going inside.

Q: What about public holidays? Is there a surcharge?
A: No surcharge on public holidays.

Q: Can we meet before we go away?
A: Absolutely! At Mind My Pet we prefer to meet our clients prior to them leaving.

Q: Will my dog be exercised?
A: Short walks are included in the regular service and long walks can be catered for.

Q: What about my fish, will you feed my fish?
A: Of course, we can feed the fish.

Q: When do I pay?
A: Deposit is made at time of booking and final payment is completed prior to your departure.

Q: What information do you need from us?
A: Have available:
• current Vet information
• emergency contact while you’re away
• important information on feeding, exercise and toileting routines

Q: What else do we need to leave?
A: Leave out:
• enough food for the time you’re away
• food and water bowls
• poop bags
• favourite toys
• treats, if used
• any medications required by the pet/s

Q: What if I need to cancel my booking?
A: Contact us as soon as possible on 1300 697 380. No cancellation fee if booking is cancelled 48 hours prior, less than that sees 50% refund given.

Q: We have 3 pets, do we get a discount?
A: You sure do! Ask about our multi-pet discount (3 pets or more discount) 30% discount on total, then an extra 10% discount on 4 or more pets.

Q: Is there a minimum number of days to book?
A: No, book just one or several.
